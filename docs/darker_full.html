SMOTools
    Info
    This tool compares the small differences in top runners movements. It's intended to be used as a reference both to learn what the "fastest" realistic movement is, as well as for the top runners themselves, to learn where their time save is. I do think that one of the best parts of SMO is finding the movement that suits you best, so if you're new to Darker, please just play around with it before studying these clips too hard.

    Click on any split to expand it into moon to moon timings, and then click on a time to go to the clip. The clips automatically link to the youtube/twitch video at the proper timestamp. This may be a bit slow to load depending on your computer.

    Times are aggregated both per-runner, and, in some cases, across runners. All timings are automatically generated, and may be off by a frame or two in some cases, and in rare cases may be off by a few seconds.

    Times are colored as follows:
    • Green: Best
    • Yellow: Less than a second slower than Best
    • Red: More than a second slower than Best

    There's a few "todos" and "won't fixes", but I'm planning to take a break for a bit before I tackle those. I spend a lot of time working on this, so I hope it's useful to you.
